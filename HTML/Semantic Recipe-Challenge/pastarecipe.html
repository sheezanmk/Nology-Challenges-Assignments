Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

About this Dish

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a traditional Italian pasta dish from Naples. It's made with just a few simple ingredients: garlic, olive oil, chilli flakes, and parsley. Perfect for a quick and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garluc
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp red chilli flakes
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until it softens
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saute the garlic until golden
  3. Add chilli flakes and a ladle of pasta water
  4. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix well
  5. Garnish with fresh parsleyand serve hot
